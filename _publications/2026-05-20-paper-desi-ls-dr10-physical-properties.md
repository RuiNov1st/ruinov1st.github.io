Galaxy physical properties, including star formation rate, stellar mass, and gas-phase metallicity, are essential for population studies and galaxy evolution analyses. This work introduces a multimodal deep learning model that combines optical images with photometric catalog features to estimate these quantities for low-redshift galaxies.

Applied to DESI Legacy Imaging Surveys DR10, the model produces a value-added catalog for approximately 547 million galaxies with redshifts z <= 0.5, providing a homogeneous photometry-based resource for statistical studies of galaxies in the local Universe.
